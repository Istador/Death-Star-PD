Come to the dark side, we have cookies.

They come in bulk and seek destruction, but they made one mistake: challenging you. The Imperial Military needs you in its campaign against the disreputable rebels that aim to destroy your deathstar. Will you join the fight?

Deathstar PD allows you to build and upgrade towers that will attack enemies that come in range. Limited credits and cookies (also known as energy) - the main ressources in the game - forces you to make meaningful decissions and plan ahead where and what to construct. 17 waves of enemies, that are constantly getting stronger and more numerous, are awaiting you.
